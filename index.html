A Waterloo Mechatronics Project

A card wizard to automatically sort your cards, hands-free!

Learn more

Automatic Sorting

Hassle-free sorting of your entire trading card collection! Vacuum grippers handle your cards gently, so there is no need to worry about scuffs or damage.

User Defined Categories

Sort your collection into up to six categories including colour, price, and foil effect!

Online Collection Database

Keep track of your collection on the go with a persistent online record of all your cards! See the value of your collection and plan out trades wherever you are.
