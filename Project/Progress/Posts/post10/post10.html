The Dragon Stopper

March 11, 2019 by Misha

There may be no better way to make sure that your swivel head knows where to go than to attach a dragon for it to run into.

This will hopefully be the last thing that needs to be 3D printed for the tower. The Dragon Stopper will serve as a platform for the limit switch that stops the vacuum head over the bucket tree. Fellow classmates, Zhongyi Wei and Sudharsan Venkatesan, piggy-backed my 3D printing booking which I'm more than okay with because sharing is caring.
